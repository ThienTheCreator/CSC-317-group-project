FAQ:

  • Does SF kitchen work 24/7?
    Duh...You already know! !

  • Is there any delivery fee?
    Yes, we charge a small amount to cover transportation expenses to our drivers but the first delivery will always be free!

  • How does drone delivery work?
    Drones are controlled by gps from our server, you will get a text once our drone is outside you house.

  • Are you open for outdoor dining?
    Yes, and we closely follow SF's guidelines to provide a safe experience for everyone. Customers and staff must wear masks,
    remain 6 feet apart whenever possible, and may only dine in with a maximum party of 6.
